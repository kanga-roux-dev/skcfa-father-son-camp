St Kevin's College Fathers Association — Thank You

Year 2–4 Father / Male Mentor & Son Camp  |  21st/22nd March 2026

Thank You
For Attending
Phillip Island Adventure Resort (PAIR)

The SKCFA is a vibrant and energetic group of fathers/guardians at St Kevin's, aiming to:

  • Foster a sense of community amongst the parents of St Kevin's College; and
  • Contribute to the development of the boys' relationship with their fathers/guardians through organised activities all year round.

The SKCFA contributes to every year level via events held for students and for every father/male mentor via e.g. dinners. We also help at school functions, again, being an active part of the college community.

If you too want to take part in your son's year level events, or other college functions, please reach out to us:
fathersassociation@stkevins.vic.edu.au

Keep up to date with all our activities via the college portal and our Facebook page:
https://www.facebook.com/groups/SKCFathersAssociation/

FOR EVERY + FATHER SON IN EVERY

IT'S YOUR FATHERS ASSOCIATION.

Take part in the rewarding opportunity to be part of your son's school and schooling life.

The Fathers Association holds events for every son and every father in every year level. It's a connected network that contributes to the strengthening of the boys' relationship with their fathers.

The Association also strengthens the network between fathers/guardians. If you'd like to be part of it in any way, contact us.
