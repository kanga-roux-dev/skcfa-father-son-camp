St Kevin's College Fathers Association — Camp Packing Guide

Year 2–4 Father / Male Mentor & Son Camp  |  21st/22nd March 2026  |  Phillip Island Adventure Resort (PAIR)

The following list can be used as a guide when packing for camp. Please label all personal belongings — we cannot be responsible for lost items. Your group leader may suggest additional items to pack or leave at home.

Clothing
  • Underwear (socks, jocks, thermals)
  • Comfortable clothing for activities
  • Long pants & long sleeved shirt
  • Jumper
  • Pajamas
  • Swimwear (if applicable)
  • Rain jacket (waterproof)
  • Gloves, Beanie, Warm Jacket (seasonal)
Required Bedding
  • Bottom Fitted Sheet
  • Pillowslip (pillow provided)
  • Sleeping bag or Doona
Footwear
  • Comfortable closed-toed shoes for activities
  • Closed-toed shoes for water activities (if applicable)
Toiletries
  • Toothbrush & Toothpaste
  • Shampoo & Conditioner
  • Soap
  • Bath Towel
Other Essentials
  • Drink Bottle
  • Sunscreen, Sun Hat & Sunglasses
  • Beach / Pool Towel (if applicable)
  • Torch & Insect Repellent
  • Laundry / Plastic Bag for dirty / wet clothes
  • Regular Medication
What NOT to Pack
  • Nuts or foods containing nuts
  • Pets or Animals (unless required as mobility aid)
  • Cooking Appliances
  • Craft Supplies (without discussion)
  • Drugs & Alcohol, Illegal Materials
Additional Items for Group Leaders
  • First Aid Kits (groups are required to supply their own)
  • Rooming Allocations & Duty Group lists
  • AV, Sport, or other Equipment needed for your Camp Program (beyond what the Resort provides)
  • Special Diet Info — submit all diet requests 14 days prior to camp

St Kevin's College Fathers Association — Thank You

Year 2–4 Father / Male Mentor & Son Camp  |  21st/22nd March 2026

Thank You
For Attending
Phillip Island Adventure Resort (PAIR)

The SKCFA is a vibrant and energetic group of fathers/guardians at St Kevin's, aiming to:

  • Foster a sense of community amongst the parents of St Kevin's College; and
  • Contribute to the development of the boys' relationship with their fathers/guardians through organised activities all year round.

The SKCFA contributes to every year level via events held for students and for every father/male mentor via e.g. dinners. We also help at school functions, again, being an active part of the college community.

If you too want to take part in your son's year level events, or other college functions, please reach out to us:
fathersassociation@stkevins.vic.edu.au

Keep up to date with all our activities via the college portal and our Facebook page:
https://www.facebook.com/groups/SKCFathersAssociation/
